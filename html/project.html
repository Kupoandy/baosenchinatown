The Baosen China Town Project

A City Within a City - Redefining Urban Living in Port Moresby

Project Overview

Baosen China Town Port Moresby is a groundbreaking 11-hectare mixed-use development strategically located at the intersection of Hubert Murry Highway and Boroko Drive, opposite 5 Mile Mobil service station Road. This master-planned community represents more than K5 Billon of investment in Port Moresby's urban infrastructure, creating a vibrant, self-contained ecosystem for living, working, and leisure.
